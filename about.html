About SpeedPulse

Built for real answers

We believe everyone deserves to understand their internet connection — not just see random numbers.

Our mission

SpeedPulse was built out of frustration with speed tests that show inflated, unreliable numbers. We use real HTTP fetch timing against Cloudflare's global infrastructure — the same network powering millions of websites — so your results are meaningful, not marketing.

Everything runs in your browser. No accounts, no apps, no data collection. Just honest diagnostics.
